About Us

The Akshaya Patra Foundation is a non-profit organization that strives to eliminate classroom hunger by implementing the Mid-Day Meal Scheme in government and government-aided schools. We also aim to counter malnutrition and support the right to education of socio-economically disadvantaged children.
